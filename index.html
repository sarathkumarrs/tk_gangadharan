Official Website Of TK Gangadharan

TK Gangadharan is a renowned Malayalam poet noted for the uniqueness and contemporaneity of his poetry. He was born in Kodungaloor as the son of Kannapan and Devaki. He started writing poetry during his childhood.

47 YEARS OF CREATIVITY

Creativity of E. Harikumar was distilled in the crucible of his life. The symbiosis of his outlook and creativity gave birth to some of the finest fiction in Malayalam.
